# Real Estate Photography Website Content Strategy

## Brand Positioning
**Mackenzie James Media** - Professional Real Estate Photography

### Value Proposition
"Helping real estate agents sell properties faster with stunning photography that showcases every home's best features."

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Real estate agents and brokers
- **Secondary**: Property developers, Airbnb hosts
- **Geographic**: [Your service area - update this]

## Website Content Structure

### Homepage
**Hero Section:**
- Headline: "Stunning Real Estate Photography That Sells Properties Faster"
- Subheadline: "Professional photography services for real estate agents who want to stand out and close deals quickly"
- CTA: "View Portfolio" + "Get Quote"

**Services Overview:**
- Professional Property Photography
- Virtual Tours & 360° Views
- Drone/Aerial Photography
- Twilight/Dusk Photography
- Commercial Real Estate Photography

**Social Proof:**
- Client testimonials
- Number of properties photographed
- Average days on market reduction

### Services Page Content

#### Interior Photography
- Showcase room layouts and flow
- Highlight natural light and space
- Professional staging advice included

#### Exterior & Aerial Photography
- Curb appeal that stops scrollers
- Neighborhood context shots
- Seasonal timing recommendations

#### Virtual Tours
- 360° immersive experiences
- Matterport-style walkthroughs
- Perfect for remote buyers

#### Twilight Photography
- Premium evening shots
- Dramatic lighting effects
- Perfect for luxury listings

### Portfolio Organization
**Categories to Create in Webflow CMS:**
- Residential Properties
- Luxury Homes
- Commercial Properties
- Condos/Townhomes
- Before/After Transformations

**For Each Portfolio Item:**
- Property type and location
- Challenge/brief
- Photography approach
- Results (days on market, sale price vs. asking)

### About Page Content
- Your photography background
- Real estate market knowledge
- Equipment and techniques
- Why photography matters in real estate sales

### Contact Page
- Service areas map
- Booking calendar integration
- Pricing guide download
- Emergency/rush job contact

What Realtors Look For in a Photographer

1. Quality & Style

  • ✔ HDR photos that meet MLS standards
  • ✔ Portfolio of recent listings (photos, video, 360° tours)
  • ✔ Extras like floor plans and virtual tours

2. Turnaround & Reliability

  • ✔ Fast delivery (same-day or next-day)
  • ✔ Flexible scheduling for urgent shoots
  • ✔ Clear communication and punctuality

3. Pricing & Added Value

  • ✔ Transparent package and add-on pricing
  • ✔ Value-added services (property websites, social templates)
  • ✔ Competitive rates for quality work
